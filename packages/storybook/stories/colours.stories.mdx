# Colours

Colours are split into two groups:

- The NICE palette ([\_settings-colours-nice.scss](https://github.com/nice-digital/nice-design-system/blob/main/components/nds-core/scss/settings/_settings-colours-nice.scss))
- Semantic colours ([\_settings-colours-semantic.scss](https://github.com/nice-digital/nice-design-system/blob/main/components/nds-core/scss/settings/_settings-colours-semantic.scss))

The NICE palette is grouped into primary, secondary and tertiary palettes.

The semantic colours take these colours and apply them to specific scenarios, e.g. link colour, border colours etc.
